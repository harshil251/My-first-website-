Switzerland

Switzerland is a country located in the confluence of Western, Central, and Southern Europe. It is a federal republic composed of 26 cantons, with Bern as the seat of the federal authorities. Switzerland is a multilingual country with four official languages: German, French, Italian, and Romansh. It has a population of around 8.5 million people, and its capital and largest city is Zurich. The country has a long history of armed neutrality—it has not been in a state of war internationally since 1815—and is home to numerous international organizations, including the Red Cross, the United Nations, and the European Free Trade Association.
